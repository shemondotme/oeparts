# OEMHub - Next Priority Features

**5টি Top Features কমপ্লিট হয়েছে, এখন কী কী করতে পারি?**

---

## 📊 SUGGESTED NEXT FEATURES (Priority Order)

### **HIGH IMPACT - করতে হবে**

#### 1. **Admin Dashboard Analytics** ⭐⭐⭐
**Why**: ম্যানেজমেন্ট এর জন্য critical
- Daily/weekly/monthly revenue chart
- Top selling products
- Order status breakdown
- New orders count
- User registration trends
- Inventory status overview
- Search popular OEM numbers

**Estimated Time**: 3-4 days
**Database**: Minimal changes
**Test Impact**: Medium

---

#### 2. **Product Bulk Actions & Export** ⭐⭐⭐
**Why**: Admin efficiency
- Bulk edit (activate/deactivate, price update, description)
- Bulk delete (soft delete)
- CSV export (products, manufacturers, cross-references)
- CSV import (batch product upload)
- Batch status updates
- Bulk VAT recalculation

**Estimated Time**: 3-4 days
**Database**: No changes
**Test Impact**: High

---

#### 3. **Inventory & Stock Management** ⭐⭐⭐
**Why**: Business critical
- Stock alerts (low stock threshold)
- Stock history/audit trail
- Restock orders from suppliers
- Stock forecast (based on sales velocity)
- Auto-archive when out of stock (optional)
- Stock level by location/warehouse

**Estimated Time**: 4-5 days
**Database**: 2-3 new tables needed
**Test Impact**: High

---

#### 4. **Customer Reviews & Ratings** ⭐⭐
**Why**: SEO + Trust building
- Reviews on products (5-star + text)
- Review moderation (admin approval)
- Verified purchase only
- Review helpful votes
- Average rating display
- Review schema (JSON-LD for SEO)

**Estimated Time**: 3-4 days
**Database**: 2 new tables
**Test Impact**: Medium

---

#### 5. **Advanced Search Filters & Facets** ⭐⭐
**Why**: Better UX, higher conversion
- Filter by condition
- Filter by manufacturer
- Filter by price range
- Filter by delivery time
- Filter by availability
- Sort by: price, newest, rating, relevance
- Save search filters

**Estimated Time**: 3-4 days
**Database**: Caching improvements
**Test Impact**: High

---

#### 6. **Admin Email Templates Manager** ⭐
**Why**: Customizable communications
- WYSIWYG editor for email templates
- Order confirmation custom text
- Order status update custom text
- Refund email custom text
- Newsletter templates
- Preview before sending
- Template version history (audit trail)

**Estimated Time**: 2-3 days
**Database**: New table for email templates
**Test Impact**: Medium

---

#### 7. **Wishlist / Saved Products** ⭐
**Why**: Repeat business, engagement
- Save/unsave products
- View wishlist
- Share wishlist
- Email wishlist to friend
- Wishlist notifications (price drop, back in stock)
- Anonymous wishlist (not logged in)

**Estimated Time**: 2-3 days
**Database**: 1 new table
**Test Impact**: Low

---

#### 8. **Order Notes & Comments** ⭐
**Why**: Better support coordination
- Admin can add internal notes on orders
- Customer-visible comments
- Notification when note added
- Note timeline view
- Search orders by notes

**Estimated Time**: 1-2 days
**Database**: 1 new table
**Test Impact**: Low

---

#### 9. **Advanced Reporting** ⭐
**Why**: Business insights
- Revenue reports (by date range, manufacturer, category)
- Customer reports (lifetime value, repeat customers)
- Product performance (best sellers, low sellers)
- Geographic reports (sales by country)
- Payment method breakdown
- Export reports (PDF/Excel)

**Estimated Time**: 4-5 days
**Database**: No changes
**Test Impact**: Medium

---

#### 10. **Discount Codes & Promotions** ⭐⭐
**Why**: Sales driver
- Create discount codes (% or fixed amount)
- Usage limits (per code, per user)
- Expiry dates
- Category/product restrictions
- Minimum order value requirement
- One-time vs reusable
- Track usage analytics

**Estimated Time**: 3-4 days
**Database**: 2 new tables
**Test Impact**: High

---
